The first set of graphs shown, affordability, demonstrates the general affordability, 
in terms of housing monthly cost, by number of bedrooms in each city.

The second set of graphs displays the line graphs of Zillow monthly prices on average per month vs Airbnb average price per month 
in 2016 Austin, demonstrating the relative lack of trend in the short term

The third and final set of graphs shows the average crossover rate in Portland over time, demonstrating that starting in 2016 the average
days per month needed for an airbnb to rent out in order to meet the same cost per month as Zillow decreased sharply from January 
until June in 2016, and stagnated from there, potentially showing a self stabilizing effect
